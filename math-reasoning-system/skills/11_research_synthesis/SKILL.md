# Skill: Research Synthesis

**Goal:** Consolidate exploratory work into a research brief with questions, evidence, conjectures, obstacles, and next actions.

**Instructions:**
1. **Gather Artifacts:** Read `artifacts/01_problem_formalization.md`, `artifacts/02_research_questions.md`, `artifacts/02_literature_review.md` if present, and `artifacts/04_research_trace.md`.
2. **Separate Status Levels:**
   - proven or source-backed facts;
   - plausible conjectures;
   - refuted candidates;
   - open questions;
   - examples and counterexamples.
3. **State Candidate Theorems:** For each surviving conjecture, write a precise theorem-style statement and list missing proof obligations.
4. **Prioritize Next Work:** Recommend 3-5 next probes or proof attempts.
5. **Avoid Overclaiming:** Do not present conjectural claims as established results.

**Output:** Save to `artifacts/05_research_brief.md`. If structured output is requested, use `schema.json`.
